# Service Policy
This Service Policy outlines the responsibilities of both vendors and customers to ensure smooth and professional event execution.
## 🔹 Vendor Responsibilities:
  * Deliver service on time and as described in their profile
  * Maintain quality standards and professional behavior
  * Inform the customer in case of delay or any issue
  * Collect remaining payment (70%) only after service is completed


## 🔹 Customer Responsibilities:
  * Provide accurate event details and cooperate with vendor
  * Pay the remaining amount after event if satisfied with service
  * Contact EventDhara within 48 hours in case of complaints


EventDhara does not take responsibility for service delays caused by natural issues (e.g., traffic, rain, etc.), but we will help resolve any complaint fairly.
