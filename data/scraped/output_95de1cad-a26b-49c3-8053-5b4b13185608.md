# Help Center
Welcome to EventDhara Help & Guide Section
We’re here to assist you in understanding how to use our platform smoothly — from browsing services to making a booking and contacting vendors.
What you can do on EventDhara:
  * Explore event services like decorators, DJs, photographers, and more
  * Filter services by location, category, theme, and budget
  * View vendor profiles with photos, ratings, and pricing
  * Book a service by paying a small advance (30%)
  * Contact vendor after booking for event discussion
  * Track your booking via email or dashboard


For help, you can contact: connect@eventdhara.in 
Live chat available Mon–Sat: 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM
