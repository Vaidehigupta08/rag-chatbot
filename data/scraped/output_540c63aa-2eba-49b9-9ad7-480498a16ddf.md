# How to Book a Service
Booking a service on EventDhara is easy and secure:
🟩 Step 1: Browse Services
Use our search bar or filter by event type, location, or vendor category.
🟩 Step 2: View Vendor Profiles
Check photos, pricing, and previous work. Compare and shortlist.
🟩 Step 3: Book Now
Click “Book Now” and fill out your event details (date, time, address, etc.).
🟩 Step 4: Pay 30% Advance
Secure your booking by paying a small advance online (via UPI, card, or net banking).
🟩 Step 5: Booking Confirmation
You’ll receive a confirmation email/SMS, and the vendor will be notified.
🟩 Step 6: Final Payment & Event
After the event is successfully delivered, you pay the remaining 70% directly to the vendor.
Need help while booking? WhatsApp or email us anytime.
