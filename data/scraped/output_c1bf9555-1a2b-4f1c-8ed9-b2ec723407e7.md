# Privacy Policy for Event Deal
**Effective Date: 01/05/2025**  
At EventDhara (“we”, “our”, or “us”), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website, mobile app, or services (together referred to as the “Platform”).
## 1. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information:
  * Personal Information: name, email address, phone number, billing details.
  * Booking Information: event type, vendor selection, date/time.
  * Device & Log Data: IP address, browser info, device type.
  * Payment Info: via third-party providers like Razorpay; we don’t store your card details.


## 2. How We Use Your Information
  * To process bookings and payments
  * To connect users with event service vendors
  * To send updates, confirmations, and support messages
  * For analytics and platform improvement


## 3. Sharing of Information
  * We do not sell or rent your information.
  * Vendor contact info is shared with users only after confirmed booking.
  * We may disclose information if legally required.


## 4. Cookies & Tracking
We use cookies and tracking tools (Google Analytics, Clarity) to understand user behavior and improve UX.
## 5. Data Security
We use SSL encryption and secure hosting. However, online data transmission can never be 100% secure.
## 6. Your Rights
You may request data access or deletion at any time by emailing us at connect@eventdhara.in 
## 7. Updates
This policy may change. Changes will be updated here with revised dates.
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: connect@eventdhara.in
