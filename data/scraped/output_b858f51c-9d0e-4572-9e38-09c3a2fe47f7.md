# EventDhara – Cancellation & Refund Policy
At EventDhara, we value both our customers and vendors. Our policy ensures fairness, user flexibility, and vendor security.
## 📌 Case A – Advance Booking (30% Paid at Time of Booking)
✔ User pays **30% upfront** to confirm booking. ✔ Vendor instantly receives **20%** of total order. ✔ Remaining amount stays with EventDhara until event day.
Cancellation Time | Refund to User | Vendor Payout | EventDhara Share  
---|---|---|---  
❌ Within 24 hrs (if event is <24 hrs away) | No Refund | ✅ 20% of total order | Platform fee  
⏳ 24–48 hrs before event | ✅ 20% of advance (≈6%) | ✅ 20% of total order | Balance stays  
⏳ 48–72 hrs before event | ✅ 50% of advance (≈15%) | ✅ 20% of total order | Balance stays  
⏳ 3–7 days before event | ✅ 70% of advance (≈21%) | ✅ 20% of total order | Balance stays  
✅ 7+ days before event | ✅ 100% of advance (minus gateway fee) | ✅ 20% of total order | EventDhara bears operational cost  
## 📌 Case B – Full Payment (100% Paid at Once)
✔ Out of full amount, **20%** instantly goes to vendor. ✔ Remaining 80% stays with EventDhara until after the event.
Cancellation Time | Refund to User | Vendor Payout | EventDhara Share  
---|---|---|---  
❌ Within 24 hrs (if event <24 hrs away) | No Refund | ✅ 20% of total order | ✅ 80% (operational)  
⏳ 24–48 hrs before event | ✅ 64% of total order | ✅ 20% | ✅ 16%  
⏳ 48–72 hrs before event | ✅ 40% of total order | ✅ 20% | ✅ 40%  
⏳ 3–7 days before event | ✅ 56% of total order | ✅ 20% | ✅ 24%  
✅ 7+ days before event | ✅ 80% of total order | ✅ 20% | ❌ Only gateway fee deducted  
###  Important Points to Remember
  * Gateway charges are non-refundable.
  * If cancelled within 24 hrs of event, no refund is possible.
  * Vendor must confirm availability before accepting booking.
  * User should verify all details before booking.
  * EventDhara ensures fairness for both parties.
