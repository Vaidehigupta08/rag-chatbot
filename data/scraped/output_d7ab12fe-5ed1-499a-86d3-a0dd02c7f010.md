# Delivery
Since EventDhara offers event services, not physical products, “delivery” refers to successful execution of the booked service at your chosen venue/date.
## What “delivery” means on EventDhara:
  * Vendor arrives at your event location on the booked date & time
  * Service is performed as agreed (e.g., decoration setup, DJ performance)
  * Delivery time may vary depending on service type (some come 1 hour before, others may set up hours earlier)


## What You Should Ensure:
  * Provide correct address, time, and phone number during booking
  * Keep in touch with the vendor after confirmation
  * Inform us immediately in case of delay or no-show


Our support team will assist in real-time if any issue arises.
